SMART WATER AND NUTRIENT DELIVERY SYSTEM FOR HYDROPONICS

Our project is a simple hydroponics farming system created by college students as a sustainable substitute for conventional farming methods. Without the need for soil, crops may be grown due to its three parallel PVC pipes with uniformly sized planting holes. Water rich in nutrients flows through the system, directly nourishing each plant's roots. This technique is modest, effective, and ideal for small-scale or urban farming.
